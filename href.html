Janak Lal Sastri Prithivi Narayan Shah Gandhi
He is Janak Lal, an old man living in Baneshwor under the bridge.
Prithvi Narayan Shah was the last king of the Gorkha Kingdom and the first king of the Kingdom of Nepal (also called the Kingdom of Gorkha).
Civil rights activist in South Africa (1893–1914) · Immediately upon arriving in South Africa, Gandhi faced discrimination due to his skin colour and heritage.
